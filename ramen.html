Ash's Banging Ramen

This is my very own take on what I believe to be a Japanese Ramen.

Stick to the recipe and directions and you won't go far wrong. Substitute the Tofu for your main of choice.

Ingredients

  • 100g ramen noodles
  • 3 large cubes of firm tofu
  • 1 portobello mushroom
  • 2 Spring onions
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon worcester sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dashi paste
  • 1 bowl water
  • salt and pepper

Directions

  1. Dissolve dashi paste in small amout of water.
  2. Bring bowl of water for ramen to boil.
  3. Bring a large pot of water for the noodles to boil and then add noodles til cooked
  4. Clean and chop spring onions into two pieces. Leave bottom half whole but finely slice top half and add to water.
  5. Roughly chop garlic and add to water.
  6. Mix in soy, worcester and fish sauce.
  7. Once water boils add tofu.
  8. Dice portobello mushroom into large cubes, add to ramen
  9. Add oil and soy to bottom of empty bowl.
  10. Drain noodles once cooked and add to bowl with oil and soy.
  11. Add ramen soup to noodles. Add remaining spring onion to soup.
  12. Add chilli flakes and more soy to taste.

Serve and enjoy
